Viral diseases affecting Groundnut!

Groundnut is susceptible to various viral diseases that can significantly impact yield and quality. Understanding these diseases is crucial for effective management and control.

Common Viral Diseases Of Groundnut

  • Groundnut Rosette Virus (GRV)
  • Groundnut Bud Necrosis(GYMV)

Table of Viral Diseases

Disease Pathogen Control measure
Groundnut Rosette Virus (GRV) virus Use heavy seed rate and rogue out infected plants periodically
Groundnut Bud Necrosis(GYMV) Virus Intercrop with cereals like pearl millet
